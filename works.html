joji

digital illustration, 2020

A simple monochromatic digital illustration I made in dedication to one of my favorite artists, Joji. His personal style, range in genres, and most of all his journey from from a renowned YouTuber to pursuing his passion as a music artist I find so inspiring. His dedication to his own passions motivate me to keep chasing my dreams, so a lot of my artwork is a testament to him.

untitled

photograph, 2023

During my photography class, I decided to experiment with projecting various images, textures, and patterns onto my subject to create unique compositions. I ended up shooting this photograph which perfectly encapsulates the grungey, mysterious and edgy aesthetic I was striving for. This photo is a true representation of my personal style and my love for experimentation in all of my work.

lost in translation

graphite portrait, 2021

This piece is a recreation of a scene from one of my favorite films, "Lost in Translation". The characters in the film are struggling with questions of identity and purpose, as they navigate through a new and unfamiliar environment. Similarly, in life, we often find ourselves in periods of transition, moving from one phase to the next, and grappling with feelings of uncertainty and ambiguity which I wanted to convey through this piece.

window

vector portrait, 2022

I created this editorial-style vector portrait during highschool for my senior graphic design class summative. I was tasked with creating a layout for a music artist of our choosing that creatively incorporates the lyrics of one of their songs. Joji has been a big inspiration for a lot of my life, hence why I made this portrait.
